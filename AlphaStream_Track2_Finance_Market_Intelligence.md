# Track 2: Finance & Market Intelligence

**Tagline:** Unlock the web for the teams that make financial decisions.

---

## Overview

Pricing, risk, procurement, and planning depend on data that reflects right now — not last week's export. It's all out there, continuously updated, rarely structured. The teams that get to it first, reliably, win.

---

## Focus Areas

This track focuses on systems that:

- Ingest and structure live financial web data into analytical workflows
- Combine signals from multiple live sources into structured intelligence objects
- Deliver alternative data at the coverage and frequency real decisions require
- Give AI agents the live financial context they need to reason over current reality

---

## Who This Track Is For

This track is for builders working on:

- **Alternative data pipelines** aggregating job postings, pricing trends, and web traffic signals
- **Filing and regulatory monitors** that detect, parse, and deliver structured alerts
- **Competitive pricing systems** tracking price changes across markets in real time
- **Earnings intelligence tools** cross-referencing web signals to surface pre-earnings indicators
- **Supplier and vendor risk systems** monitoring financial health and leadership signals at scale
- **Multi-source synthesis engines** delivering structured company or sector intelligence objects
